My name is Juan Carlos Calvo Esquivel, and I've spent over 30 years walking the trails of Monteverde's cloud forest. For me, this isn't a job — it's a calling. I grew up surrounded by the mist and the music of the bellbird, and every day I discover something new in these forests.

I guide in both English and Spanish, and my goal is simple: to help you see what most people walk right past.

Night Tour — Refugio Monteverde

Discover Monteverde after dark. 80% of the cloud forest's wildlife is nocturnal — and this is your chance to witness it. Red-eyed tree frogs, pit vipers, sloths, armadillos, sleeping toucans, and fireflies. An experience unlike anything you've seen before.
