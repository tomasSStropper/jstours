Juan Carlos Calvo Esquivel

My name is Juan Carlos Calvo Esquivel. I was born in the mountains of Costa Rica, and I have never left — not because I couldn't, but because I found everything worth finding right here. For over 30 years I have walked these trails, watching the forest change with the seasons, listening to birds that most people will never hear in their lifetime. I guide in English and Spanish, and I bring one thing that no app or tour company can offer: a lifetime of paying attention.

Night Tour — Refugio Monteverde
